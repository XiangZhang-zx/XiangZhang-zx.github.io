Research

1st Author · Advisor: Dr. Dongfang Liu

Diffusion vs. Autoregressive Language Models

Conducted the first systematic comparison of diffusion and autoregressive language models across 20+ benchmarks at 472M, 1B, and 8B scale. Demonstrated that diffusion models outperform AR models on multi-constraint tasks (GSM8K, LogiQA, ANLI) while AR retains advantages on single-constraint benchmarks.

1st Co-Author · Advisor: Dr. Dongfang Liu

Diffusion Language Models for Nuclear Fusion Prediction

Proposed ICF-DLM, a diffusion language model framework for nuclear fusion yield prediction at the National Ignition Facility. Designed a physics-inspired hierarchical decomposition strategy and achieved state-of-the-art performance on ICF experimental datasets.
